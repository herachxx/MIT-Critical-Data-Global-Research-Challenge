About ISRC

A Global Platform for Rising Minds

The International Scholars Research Competition celebrates young researchers pushing the boundaries of human knowledge. We provide a prestigious platform to showcase rigorous, original research across multiple disciplines.

Whether your research focuses on AI, biology, physics, medicine, or social sciences, ISRC 2026 welcomes submissions from students worldwide. Compete fairly, gain recognition, and potentially see your work published in leading academic journals.
