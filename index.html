About ISRC

A Competition Built on
Academic Excellence

The International Scholars Research Competition has been the premier stage for student researchers since 2014. We connect ambitious scholars with a global network of academics, industry leaders, and funding opportunities.

Every year, participants from over 48 countries submit original research across science, technology, humanities, and beyond — all judged by a distinguished international panel.
